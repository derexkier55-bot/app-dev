

# **The Chronicles of Narnia**

*The Chronicles of Narnia* is a **fantasy series** written by *C.S. Lewis*. It takes readers into a magical world full of adventure, bravery, and friendship.

---

## **Main Characters**

1. Peter Pevensie                                                                                                       
2. Susan Pevensie                                                      
3. Edmund Pevensi  
4. Lucy Pevensie  

---

### **Favorite Quotes**

> "Once a king or queen of Narnia, always a king or queen of Narnia."

---

### **Interesting Facts**

* The series has **7 books**.
* The first published book was *The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe*.
* Aslan represents *goodness and hope*.
